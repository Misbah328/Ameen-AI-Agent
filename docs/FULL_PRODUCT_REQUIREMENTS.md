# Full Product Requirements

## Vision

Ameen Secretary will become an Enterprise AI Executive Operating System that manages the complete executive workflow before, during, and after meetings.

The platform will combine AI agents, meeting intelligence, board governance, executive knowledge management, workflow automation, analytics, and organizational memory into a single system.
